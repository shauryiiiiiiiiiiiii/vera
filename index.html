About

Veraitor

Veraitor is an online photo editor which helps you to edit photos with some cool modules and you can also add custom watermark to your photos and it's easy to use. [PC ONLY] You can see the shortcuts listed on the shortcut menu to make your editing even more faster.

Keyboard shortcuts

Vera offers keyboard shortcuts for common functions. These may not work with all browserson all devices. Also, they will not work if any popups (like the watermark editor or this help menu) are open.

Command Shortcut

Important info for Safari

Safari 13.1+ on macOS, as well as Safari on iOS/iPadOS 13.4+, introduced a seven-day cap on all locally-stored data by websites. This means that unless you open Vera at least once a week, your saved watermarks could be deleted.

The only way to avoid losing your watermarks is to export them as files (from the watermark list), then re-import them after Safari wipes Vera's data.

In addition, Vera may not be able to download files (completed images, exported watermarks, etc.) when it is opened from the iOS home screen. To get around this in your browser instead.
